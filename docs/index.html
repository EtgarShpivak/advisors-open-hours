Why We're Doing This

Building the future — together, through uncertainty.

Israel's startup ecosystem has always punched above its weight. But right now, our founders are navigating uniquely difficult circumstances — managing teams through uncertainty, adapting go-to-market strategies to a changed world, and building companies while the country faces extraordinary challenges.

We're a group of senior marketing professionals — ex-CMOs, VPs, and GTM leads from Israel's most successful tech companies. We believe the best response to hard times is to build stronger together. So we're each giving one hour, freely.

"The best companies in Israeli history were built in difficult moments. We're here to make sure the next ones will be too."
