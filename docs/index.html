Why We're Doing This

One hour, freely given — because the ecosystem needs it now.

We're a group of senior Israeli marketing leaders — ex-CMOs, VPs, and GTM leads from Israel's most successful tech companies. We've each been in the room when a startup found its footing. We know how much a single sharp conversation can change a trajectory.

This is our pro-bono contribution to the ecosystem. Each advisor chooses which startup they take on — based on fit, context, and where they can genuinely add value. When you submit a request, it goes straight to the advisor by email, and they decide.

"The best companies in Israeli history were built in difficult moments. We're here to make sure the next ones will be too."
